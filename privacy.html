Privacy Policy

AmazonConsultant.ae (denoted by “we,” “our,” or “us”) is all about protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy discusses how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our e-commerce automation services or even our website for surfing.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information during your interaction with our website:

Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, business details, and payment information when you sign up for our services.

Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and device details are collected through cookies and analytics tools.

Transaction Details: Information about services you purchase, invoices, and payment history is collected for order placement.

Communications: Any communication via email, chat, or support inquiries is collected and protected with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Providing and managing our automation services for Amazon, Walmart, Noon, and other platforms.

To process the payments and generate invoices.

Improving user experience and overall website functionality.

Sending service updates, promotional offers, and relevant communications.

Ensuring compliance with UAE and Gulf region laws and regulations.

3. Data Protection & Security

At our agency, we are fully concerned about implementing strict security measures to safeguard your data from any unauthorized access or misuse. This may include encryption, secure servers, and access control policies.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We are completely against the selling or sharing of your personal data with third-party authorities, except in the following conditions:

When trusted service providers are assisting us in the prompt delivery of our services (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers).

When required by UAE or Gulf region authorities for any legal matter.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are used to improve your browsing experience with AmazonConsultant.ae. Such tracking technology collects some of your information. However, you are authorized to deny the cookies. Please note that declining cookies may affect your browsing experience. You can manage the cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

Access, update, or delete your personal data.

Sign out or unsubscribe from our marketing communications.

Request data portability and modification where necessary.

If you want to exercise any of the above rights, feel free to contact us at info@amazonconsultant.ae.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that help us run our various operations smoothly. Please note that we are not responsible for their privacy practices. You are encouraged to review their policies before proceeding.

8. Updates to This Policy

The given Privacy Policy gets updated periodically. However, the latest version will always be available on our website for review.
