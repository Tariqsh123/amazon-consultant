Frequently Asked Questions About Our Book Writing Services

Get quick answers to the most common questions authors ask about our professional book writing and ghostwriting services.

How does your book writing service work?

You share your story idea, outline, or concept, and our professional ghostwriters turn it into a compelling manuscript. We collaborate with you at every stage—drafting, editing, and refining—so the final book reflects your vision and voice.

How much does professional book writing cost?

Pricing depends on length, genre, research requirements, and complexity. We offer customized packages to suit first-time authors as well as experienced professionals. A free consultation helps us create the best plan for your budget and goals.

How long does it take to complete a book?

It usually takes 8–16 weeks to complete a manuscript, depending on its length and complexity. This includes writing, editing, and revisions. Faster turnaround options are also available for urgent projects.

Will the book sound like I wrote it?

Yes! Our ghostwriters specialize in capturing your voice, tone, and personality. Through feedback and revisions, we make sure the manuscript feels authentic—like you wrote it yourself.

Do you work with new and first-time authors?

Absolutely! We love working with first-time authors. From brainstorming and outlining to editing and final polishing, we guide you step by step so your publishing journey is exciting and stress-free.

Is my book idea kept confidential?

Yes, your privacy is our priority. We provide NDAs (non-disclosure agreements) and follow strict confidentiality practices to ensure your story and ideas remain 100% secure.
