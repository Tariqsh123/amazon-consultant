How Does Your Amazon Book Consulting Service Work?

We provide end-to-end guidance for authors. From manuscript formatting, cover design, and Amazon KDP setup to keyword research, listing optimization, and marketing strategies, we ensure your book is professionally published and positioned for success.

How Much Does It Cost to Publish a Book on Amazon With Your Help?

The cost depends on the services you need—such as editing, cover design, or marketing. We offer flexible packages that fit both first-time authors and established writers. A free consultation helps us provide you with a tailored plan and pricing.

How Long Does It Take to Publish a Book on Amazon?

Once your manuscript is ready, publishing on Amazon KDP usually takes 1–2 weeks, including formatting, cover design, and listing setup. However, timelines may vary depending on whether editing or marketing campaigns are included.

Can You Help My Book Rank Higher on Amazon?

Do You Work With First-Time Authors?
